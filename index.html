Madeline is a content developer with a focus in Drupal and a strong background in Communications, higher education administration, copy editing, and project management. She graduated from Bennington College in 2016 with a BA in Literature. Her senior work was a sixty page thesis analyzing the structure of War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy. Upon graduation, she was awarded the inagural Kilpatrick Fellowship in the Office of Communications and Media Relations at Bennington College, where she worked for a year and a half until her promotion to Digital Communications Manager in January of 2018. As Digital Communications Manager, she oversees content and updates for the Bennington College website, as well as facilitates the college's internal communications. She spent her early years in California and now resides in Vermont.
